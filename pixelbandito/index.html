Daycycle

The gradient in the background is a work-in-progress.

It uses a linear gradient (with creative license) to represent a day-night cycle. The gradient is positioned vertically according to the browser's local time, and it animates up and down on a 24 hour cycle.

In addition, it uses SunCalc to determine local sunrise, solarNoon, and sunset based on the browser location; and re-calibrates the day length of the animation.
